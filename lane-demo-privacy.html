Privacy Policy — Lane Demo

Effective date: October 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Lane demo (“Demo”) processes information when you use our public Custom GPT with Actions to browse products and complete a test checkout using the Lane ACP Adapter API.

What data we process

  • Chat content you type into ChatGPT (processed by OpenAI under its policies).
  • Product choices and quantities needed to create a checkout session.
  • Contact and shipping details you explicitly provide (e.g., name, email, address) to simulate fulfillment.
  • Technical logs such as timestamps, request identifiers, and non-sensitive API metadata for reliability and abuse prevention.

We do not request or collect real payment card numbers. The Demo uses test/dummy payment method tokens in sandbox environments. Please do not enter real PANs or sensitive credentials.

How we use data

  • To call our demo APIs and show you product options and an order confirmation.
  • To operate, troubleshoot, and secure the Demo (e.g., detect abuse, ensure idempotent retries).

Sharing

We share data only with service providers that power the Demo (e.g., OpenAI to run the Custom GPT; hosting and monitoring vendors for the API). We do not sell personal information.

Retention

Demo API logs are retained for a limited period (e.g., 30–90 days) for reliability and abuse detection, then deleted or aggregated. Chat content may be retained by OpenAI per its policies.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (HTTPS, access controls, token-based auth). This Demo is for evaluation only and is not meant for production processing of real customer data or payments.

Your choices

  • Do not provide real payment details or unnecessary personal data.
  • You may request deletion of Demo API logs that can reasonably be linked to you by contacting us (see below).

Children

The Demo is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

Contact

Email: privacy@lane.example

Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

This Policy applies only to the Lane Demo. Your use of ChatGPT remains subject to OpenAI’s terms and privacy policy.
